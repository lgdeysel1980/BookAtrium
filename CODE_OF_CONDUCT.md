# BookAtrium Community Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

BookAtrium aims to provide a welcoming and respectful environment for users, contributors, and maintainers.

Examples of positive behaviour include:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Providing clear and useful feedback
- Helping others where possible
- Respecting different experience levels and viewpoints
- Protecting private and sensitive information

Unacceptable behaviour includes:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or insulting language
- Spam or repeated off-topic posting
- Publishing another person’s private information
- Deliberately misleading reports
- Uploading harmful or unlawful material

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that does not follow these standards.

Users who repeatedly disregard these standards may be restricted or blocked from participating.

## Scope

This code applies to BookAtrium Issues, Discussions, pull requests, and other official community spaces.
